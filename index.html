About



My name is Paolla Dutra and I am currently a senior at NYU doing research in web accessibility. I have experience with UX research, web development, and image processing. I am majoring in Integrated Digital Media, which is an engineering major focused on Computer Science and design. I love the front end side of Computer Science, but I am very interested in the back end too, which is why I am minoring in CS. Throughout my major, minor, and elective such as Data Science, Digital Logic, and Machine Learning, I have come across many new and emerging technologies, which I hope to use to help people in the best of my abilities.

On this website you'll find some of the projects I have worked. Once you click on a project, the skills I have used to implement it will show up under the "Skills Used" section to the left, below "Projects".
